My Teaching Experience

Over the course of Fall 2024 and Spring 2025, I had the opportunity to serve as an Undergraduate Learning Assistant (ULA) in both mathematics and computer science courses at Michigan State University.

Courses Taught

  • MTH103 – Fall 2024: Led two recitation sections with 30 students each. I guided students through problem-solving practice, administered quizzes, and proctored exams.
  • CSE102 – Spring 2025: Instructed one lab section with 35 students. I facilitated hands-on coding sessions, provided individual support, and helped reinforce core computing concepts.

Total students taught: 95

What I Learned

Teaching has significantly sharpened my communication skills and deepened my understanding of the material I was teaching. I learned how to break down complex topics into manageable parts, adapt my approach based on student feedback, and build a supportive learning environment. Watching students grow in confidence and competence was incredibly rewarding and reinforced my passion for education and mentorship.
