My Volunteering Experience

Most of my volunteer work has taken place online through Telegram, where I’ve contributed to educational communities by supporting students academically and administratively.

AlHawat Academy (2018 – Present)

  • Began as a math helper in the academy’s group chat, where I regularly explained math questions to students in real time.
  • Promoted to group administrator due to consistent support and helpfulness.
  • Eventually became admin of all 16 educational groups, each serving a unique aspect of a student’s academic path.

These groups included:

  • Dedicated groups for 10th, 11th, and 12th grade students
  • University student support groups
  • English language learning group
  • Specialized academic resources and exam prep groups

What I Gained

Volunteering taught me leadership, patience, and how to manage large communities effectively. I improved my ability to communicate clearly with students of all levels and learned how to motivate and mentor others in a digital space. Being entrusted with the responsibility of overseeing thousands of students across multiple groups was an incredible honor and strengthened my passion for education and service.
